Study of quantum mechanical systems for information processing. Topics include quantum error correction codes and simulation of Hamiltonian dynamics.

## Quantum Information Theory

Quantum information extends classical information theory by encoding data in quantum states. Entanglement provides a resource for tasks impossible in classical settings, including superdense coding and quantum teleportation.

## Error Correction

Quantum error correction codes protect quantum information against decoherence and noise. Stabilizer codes and topological codes represent the leading approaches for fault-tolerant quantum computation.

## Hamiltonian Simulation

Efficient simulation of quantum Hamiltonians on quantum computers enables the study of many-body physics, quantum chemistry, and materials science problems intractable for classical computers.
