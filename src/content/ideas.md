Early-stage research directions and exploratory concepts not yet formalized into full projects. This space captures emerging interests at the intersection of neuroscience, physics, and computing.

## Criteria for Ideas

Ideas are captured when they represent:
- Interesting intersections between established fields
- Responses to recent breakthroughs or developments
- Personal intellectual curiosity
- Potential for future project development

## Development Pipeline

Ideas progress through stages:
1. **Speculation**: Initial insight and brainstorming
2. **Literature Review**: Understanding existing work in the space
3. **Feasibility Analysis**: Assessing technical and resource requirements
4. **Prototyping**: Building proof-of-concept or simulation
5. **Project Formalization**: Transition to active research project

## Relationship to Projects

Many active projects originated as ideas in this section. The distinction is maturity level—ideas are at the exploration phase while projects have clear objectives and timelines.

## Current Focus Areas

Emerging areas of investigation:
- Bio-inspired computing architectures
- Non-invasive brain-computer interfaces
- Quantum-inspired classical algorithms
- Novel cryptographic approaches
